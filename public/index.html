What We Do

Our Core Services

From forgotten attics to weathered fences and worn concrete — we transform the spaces most contractors overlook.

Attic Flooring

That dusty attic is wasted square footage waiting to be reclaimed. We engineer and install safe, durable attic decking systems that turn your storage attic into a functional, accessible part of your home — built to code and built to last.

Stop losing tools and holiday decorations to a dark, unstable space. Walkable attic flooring adds real value to your home the smart way.

Fence Staining

Houston's humidity and blazing sun destroy untreated wood fences fast. Our professional staining service seals out moisture, blocks UV damage, and brings back that rich, just-installed look — extending the life of your fence by years.

We use premium, weatherproof stains built for the Texas climate. Cedar, pine, or composite — we match the right product to your fence type.

Small Concrete Work

When you need a new walkway, patio slab, AC pad, or a small driveway extension — we pour clean, code-compliant concrete that matches your existing slab and stands up to Houston's clay soil and weather swings.

No job too small. We handle pad extensions, garage aprons, steps, and pour-to-grade work with proper base prep and control joints.
