What We Do

Our Core Services

Let's turn that attic space into usable storage space!

We specialize in attic flooring, fence staining and small concrete jobs.

Attic Flooring

That dusty attic is wasted square footage waiting to be reclaimed. We install safe durable adequate decking.

Stop losing tools and holiday decorations to a dark, unstable space. Walkable attic flooring adds real value to your home the smart way.

Fence Staining

Houston's humidity and blazing sun destroy untreated wood fences fast. By staining your fence it'll help seal out moisture, UV damage, and dress up the backyard. It will extend the life of your fence.

We use premium, weatherproof stains built for the Texas climate. Cedar, pine, or composite — we match the right product to your fence type.

Small Concrete Work

We can add a new walkway, patio slab, trash can slab, AC pad, or a small driveway extension. Just give us a call, we are happy to do the small jobs.

No job too small. We handle pad extensions, garage aprons, steps, and pour-to-grade work with proper base prep and control joints.
