Frequently Asked Questions

Will installing attic flooring damage my ceiling or void my insulation warranty?
No. Our engineered decking system is designed to distribute load across the joists without compressing insulation. We maintain proper ventilation clearance and never compromise your existing insulation — in fact, most homeowners see improved energy efficiency after installation.
Do you use water-based or oil-based stains for fences?
We use deep-penetrating oil-based stains — specifically formulated for the Texas climate. Oil stains bond at a molecular level with the wood fiber, providing superior moisture resistance, UV protection, and longer-lasting color compared to water-based alternatives. Water-based stains sit on the surface and tend to peel and fade faster, especially in Houston's heat and humidity.
How long does it take for a new concrete patio to cure before we can use it?
Light foot traffic is safe after 24–48 hours. For heavier use — furniture, grills, foot traffic from pets — we recommend waiting 7 days. The concrete reaches full structural strength at 28 days, but your patio is ready for normal living well before that. We also apply a cure sealant at pour to maximize durability and minimize surface cracks.
Why should I choose F13 LLC over a larger general contractor?
We're a local owned small business. That means you get direct communication. We show up when we say we will, answer your texts personally, and treat every project with the discipline and pride of someone who takes absolute ownership. Larger contractors juggle dozens of jobs at once; we focus on yours.
